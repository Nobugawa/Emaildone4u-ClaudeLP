FAQ

Partner questions

Is there a cost to join the partner program?+

No — it's completely free. There are no minimums, no fees, and no obligations. You refer when it makes sense for your clients.

How are referrals tracked?+

We give you a unique referral link and/or promo code. Any client who uses your link during intake is tracked to your account automatically. You'll receive a confirmation email every time a referral converts.

When do I get paid?+

Referral fees are paid monthly for all setups completed in the prior month. You choose PayPal, Venmo, or check. Minimum payout is $50 (one completed setup).

Do referrals expire?+

No. If a client you referred signs up three months later, you still get the $50. We track referrals indefinitely.

Can I white-label this service?+

Yes — contact us to discuss white-label arrangements. This is especially popular with web designers and agencies who want to offer email setup as their own service without doing the technical work.
