FAQ

Common questions

Still on the fence? Here's what most people ask before getting started.

Do I need to already own a domain?+

No — we can purchase and register one for you as part of the setup. Domain registration typically costs $12–18/year and is billed directly to you by the registrar.

What is Google Workspace?+

Google Workspace is Google's paid business suite (~$7/month) that lets you use Gmail with your own custom domain. It includes Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar, Meet, and more — all under your brand.

What if I already use Gmail for business?+

No problem. We can set up forwarding from your old Gmail address so you don't miss any emails during the transition. Most clients run both for a week or two, then fully switch over.

How do I know my account stays secure?+

We use Google's delegated admin system — meaning we never ask for or store your password. Once setup is complete and confirmed, we remove our delegated access entirely. Your account is 100% yours.

Can I get multiple email addresses?+

Yes. Alias addresses like info@, billing@, and support@ are included at no extra charge. They all route to your main inbox — no additional mailboxes or fees.

What's included in the 30-day support?+

Any questions about your new email, help setting it up on additional devices, or minor configuration adjustments. Reach us by phone, text, or email — we respond same day.
